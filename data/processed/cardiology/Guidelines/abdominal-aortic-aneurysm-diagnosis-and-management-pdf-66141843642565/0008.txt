For more guidance, see the NICE guideline on tobacco: preventing uptake, promoting quitting and treating dependence. 1.2.2 Ensure that people with an AAA who have hypertension receive care in line with the NICE guideline on hypertension in adults. For a short explanation of why the committee made these recommendations and how they might affect practice, see the rationale and impact section on reducing the risk of rupture. Full details of the evidence and the committee's discussion are in evidence review C: risk factors associated with abdominal aortic aneurysm growth or rupture.
