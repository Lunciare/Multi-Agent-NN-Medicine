Fragility fractures of the pelvis, hip and proximal femur In March 2018, the use of LMWH and fondaparinux sodium in young people under 18 in recommendations 1.11.2 and 1.11.3 was off label. See NICE's information on prescribing medicines. 1.11.2 Offer VTE prophylaxis for a month to people with fragility fractures of the pelvis, hip or proximal femur if the risk of VTE outweighs the risk of bleeding. Choose either: • LMWH, starting 6 to12 hours after surgery or • fondaparinux sodium, starting 6 hours after surgery, providing there is low risk of bleeding. [2018] 1.11.3 Consider pre-operative VTE prophylaxis for people with fragility fractures of the pelvis, hip or proximal femur if surgery is delayed beyond the day after admission. Give the last dose no less than 12 hours before surgery for LMWH or 24 hours before surgery for fondaparinux sodium.
