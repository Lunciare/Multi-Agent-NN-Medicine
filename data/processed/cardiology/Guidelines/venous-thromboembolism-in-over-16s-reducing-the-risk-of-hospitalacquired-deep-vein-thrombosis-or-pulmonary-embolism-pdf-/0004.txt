[2018] Surgical and trauma patients 1.1.5 Assess all surgical and trauma patients to identify the risk of VTE and bleeding: • as soon as possible after admission to hospital or by the time of the first consultant review, or • in accident and emergency, if they have not been admitted within 12 hours of their arrival • using a tool published by a national UK body, professional network or peer- reviewed journal. A tool commonly used to develop a treatment plan for surgical patients is the Department of Health VTE risk assessment tool.
