Boston Scientific's HeartLogic and Latitude NXT Patient Management System work together to provide advanced monitoring and management capabilities for people with heart failure who have CIEDs. The system has daily data transfers to the clinical team. The transmitter can use a mobile phone connection or an internet connection to relay the data. The system is configured to send an alert to a healthcare professional when the index is over a set threshold (customisable by a healthcare professional). Healthcare professionals need to log on to the LATITUDE NXT website to receive alerts. Secondary notification of alerts may be through email or text message.
