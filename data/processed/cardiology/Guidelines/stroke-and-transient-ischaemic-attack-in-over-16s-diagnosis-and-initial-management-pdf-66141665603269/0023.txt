High-intensity mobilisation High-intensity mobilisation refers to the very early mobilisation intervention from the AVERT trial. (Further details of the intervention performed in the trial can be found in NICE's evidence review F: very early mobilisation.) It includes mobilisation that: • begins within the first 24 hours of stroke onset • includes at least 3 additional out-of-bed sessions compared with usual care • focuses on sitting, standing and walking (that is, out of bed) activity.
