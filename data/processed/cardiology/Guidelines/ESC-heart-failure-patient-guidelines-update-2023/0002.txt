Please disseminate it widely. How Will This Document Help Me? This document is intended to contribute to your understanding of your condition and give you the knowledge and confidence to be involved in shared decision-making with your healthcare providers regarding treatment and other aspects of your health. It also provides suggestions on ways to look after yourself, which is essential in the effective management of heart failure. 1 What is Heart Failure? Heart failure is not a single disease but a ‘syndrome’ made up of symptoms, such as breathlessness or fatigue, that may appear alongside signs such as swollen ankles, caused by something wrong in the heart.
