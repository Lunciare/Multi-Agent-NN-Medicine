[2010, amended 2019] Women who need additional fetal monitoring 1.6.11 Carry out an ultrasound for fetal growth and amniotic fluid volume assessment and umbilical artery doppler velocimetry starting at between 28 and 30 weeks (or at least 2 weeks before previous gestational age of onset if earlier than 28 weeks) and repeating 4 weeks later in women with previous: • severe pre-eclampsia • pre-eclampsia that resulted in birth before 34 weeks • pre-eclampsia with a baby whose birth weight was less than the 10th centile • intrauterine death • placental abruption. [2010] 1.6.12 In women who need additional fetal monitoring (see recommendation 1.6.11), carry out cardiotocography only if clinically indicated.
