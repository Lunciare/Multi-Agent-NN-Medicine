[2010] Nutritional supplements 1.1.5 Do not recommend the following supplements solely with the aim of preventing hypertensive disorders during pregnancy: • magnesium • folic acid • antioxidants (vitamins C and E) • fish oils or algal oils • garlic. [2010] Diet 1.1.6 Do not recommend salt restriction during pregnancy solely to prevent gestational hypertension or pre-eclampsia. [2010] Lifestyle 1.1.7 Give the same advice on rest, exercise and work to women with chronic hypertension or at risk of hypertensive disorders during pregnancy as healthy pregnant women. See the NICE guideline on antenatal care. [2010, amended 2019] Diabetes 1.1.8 For women with pre-existing diabetes or gestational diabetes, see the NICE guideline on diabetes in pregnancy. [2019] 1.2 Assessment of proteinuria in hypertensive disorders of pregnancy 1.2.1 Interpret proteinuria measurements for pregnant women in the context of a full clinical review of symptoms, signs and other investigations for pre-eclampsia. [2019] 1.2.2 Use an automated reagent-strip reading device for dipstick screening for proteinuria in pregnant women in secondary care settings.
