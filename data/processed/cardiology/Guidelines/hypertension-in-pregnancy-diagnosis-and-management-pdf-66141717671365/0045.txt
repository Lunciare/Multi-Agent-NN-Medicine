Absolute risks are not reported, because these will vary considerably, depending on the follow-up time (range from 1 to 40 years postpartum). 1.10.3 Advise women who have had a hypertensive disorder of pregnancy to discuss how to reduce their risk of cardiovascular disease, including hypertensive disorders, with their GP or specialist. This may include: • avoiding smoking, as recommended in the NICE guideline on tobacco • maintaining a healthy lifestyle, as recommended in the NICE guideline on cardiovascular disease • maintaining a healthy weight, as recommended in the NICE guideline on overweight and obesity management.
