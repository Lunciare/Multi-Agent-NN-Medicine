[2010] 1.5.17 For women with pre-eclampsia who have taken antihypertensive treatment and have given birth: • continue antihypertensive treatment (for choice of antihypertensive during the postnatal period, see the section on antihypertensive treatment during the postnatal period, including during breastfeeding) • consider reducing antihypertensive treatment if their blood pressure falls below 140/90 mmHg • reduce antihypertensive treatment if their blood pressure falls below 130/ 80 mmHg.
