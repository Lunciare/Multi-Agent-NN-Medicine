[NICE's guideline on stroke rehabilitation in adults, recommendation 1.16.4] Equality and diversity considerations Services should make reasonable adjustments to help adults with significant cognitive impairment and stroke to stay in work or education or find new employment, volunteering and educational opportunities. Some adults may be unable to work, so other occupational or education activities should be considered, including prevocational training. Quality statement 6: Regular review of rehabilitation goals Quality statement Adults who have had a stroke have their rehabilitation goals reviewed at regular intervals.
