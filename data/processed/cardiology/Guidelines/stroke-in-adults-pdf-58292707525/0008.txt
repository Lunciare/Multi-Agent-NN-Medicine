What the quality statement means for different audiences Service providers (such as secondary care providers and community care providers) ensure that adults having stroke rehabilitation are offered at least 3 hours of rehabilitation covering a range of multidisciplinary therapy for a minimum of 5 days a week. Health and social care practitioners offer adults having stroke rehabilitation at least 3 hours of rehabilitation covering a range of multidisciplinary therapy for a minimum of 5 days a week. Commissioners ensure that they commission services in which adults having stroke rehabilitation are offered at least 3 hours of rehabilitation covering a range of multidisciplinary therapy for a minimum of 5 days a week. Adults having rehabilitation therapy after a stroke are offered at least 3 hours of rehabilitation therapy that they need on at least 5 days a week.
