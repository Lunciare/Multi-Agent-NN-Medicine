What the quality statement means for different audiences Service providers (such as secondary care providers) ensure that systems are in place for adults presenting at an A&E department with suspected stroke to be admitted to a specialist acute stroke unit within 4 hours of arrival. Healthcare professionals admit adults presenting at an A&E department with suspected stroke to a specialist acute stroke unit within 4 hours of arrival. Commissioners ensure that they commission services that can demonstrate that adults presenting at A&E departments with suspected stroke are admitted to a specialist acute stroke unit within 4 hours of arrival.
