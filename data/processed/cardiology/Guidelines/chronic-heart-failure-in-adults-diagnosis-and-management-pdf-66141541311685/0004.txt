[2003] 1.1.6 The primary care team should take over routine management of heart failure as soon as it has been stabilised and its management optimised. [2018] Writing a care plan 1.1.7 The specialist heart failure MDT should write a summary for each person with heart failure that includes: • diagnosis and aetiology • medicines prescribed, monitoring of medicines, when medicines should be reviewed and any support the person needs to take the medicines • functional abilities and any social care needs • social circumstances, including carers' needs.
