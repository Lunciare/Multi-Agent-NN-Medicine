[2013] 1.12.7 Speech and language therapists should assess people experiencing severe communication difficulties after stroke to see if they could benefit from using a communication aid or other technologies (for example, home-based computer therapies or mobile apps). [2013] 1.12.8 Consider a computer-based programme (or apps) tailored to individual goals and circumstances in relation to word finding, alongside face-to- face speech and language therapy. [2023] 1.12.9 Provide communication aids to people who could benefit from them after stroke and offer training in how to use them. [2013] 1.12.10 Tell people with communication difficulties after stroke about community-based communication and support groups (such as those provided by voluntary organisations) and encourage them to participate in them.
