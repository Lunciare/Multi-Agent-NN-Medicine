The recommended starting dosage, as described in the summary of product characteristics, is 285 mg once weekly for 3 months, followed by down titration to a maintenance dosing schedule of once every 2 weeks. If there has not been a greater than 25% reduction in triglyceride levels, or if these remain above 22.6 mmol per litre at 3 months, treatment should be stopped. If response is inadequate (in terms of serum triglyceride reduction) after 6 months of treatment, an increase in dosing frequency to 285 mg once weekly should be considered. Dosing may also change at 9 months and later depending on response to treatment and platelet levels. 3.2 The adverse reactions listed as very common (that is, occurring in 1 in 10 people or more) in the summary of product characteristics for volanesorsen include thrombocytopenia and injection site reactions.
