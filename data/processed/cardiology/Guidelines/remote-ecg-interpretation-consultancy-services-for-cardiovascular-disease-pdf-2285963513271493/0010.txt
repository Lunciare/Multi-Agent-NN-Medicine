Resource consequences It is estimated that 34.7 ECGs per 1,000 patients are recorded annually in GP practices (Wolff et al. 2012). Assuming that a GP practice sends all recorded ECGs to a service for interpretation, then the likely use is about 35 ECGs per 1,000 patients.
