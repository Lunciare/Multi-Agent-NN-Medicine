She noticed it was worse lying down and when she took a deep breath but a little better when sitting up. She also had a mild fever and felt “off” for a week after a cold. The doctor heard a slight rub when listening to her heart and ordered tests. Blood tests revealed high levels of a marker of inflammation and an echocardiogram showed mild pericardial effusion.
