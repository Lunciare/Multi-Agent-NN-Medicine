The Committee also accepted the consultation suggestions that patients with acute coronary syndrome would already be receiving 12 months' treatment with clopidogrel and that no additional costs would be incurred in this population. 4.3.11 The Committee heard testimony from the clinical specialists and received information during the consultation period that in some areas procurement arrangements had resulted in a price difference of £300 for DESs over BMSs. The Committee also received information that although no nationally procured price currently existed for DESs, price differences that were less than £300 did exist in some regions.
