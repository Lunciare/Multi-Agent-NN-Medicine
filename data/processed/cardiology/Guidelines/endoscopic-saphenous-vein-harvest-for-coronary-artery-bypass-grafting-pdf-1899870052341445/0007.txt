By 3 months, the patient had recovered without any neurological sequelae. 5.6 Massive carbon dioxide (CO ) embolisation was reported in 2 patients in a case 2 series of 405 patients: 1 patient was successfully treated pharmacologically and the other needed emergency cardiopulmonary bypass support to complete the coronary artery bypass graft surgery. 5.7 A case report described scrotal distension due to CO and signs of cellulitis in 2 1 patient following endoscopic saphenous vein harvesting. The patient was treated with antibiotics and discharged after 14 days. 5.8 Pneumoperitoneum was reported in 1 patient in a case report.
