Whichever model is applied, however, all parties concerned need to acknowledge that the problem is important. In addition, it has to be possible to devise policies to remedy it, and there has to be a political willingness to act. Examples from other countries of where policy has been successfully used to reduce CVD are particularly valuable in showing what could be achieved in the UK. 1.59 This guidance has made the case unequivocally that CVD is a major and, most significantly, a preventable problem.
