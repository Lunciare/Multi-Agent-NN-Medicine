Use the findings to inform future activities. Recommendation 19 Children and young people Whose health will benefit? • Children and young people aged under 16 years. Who should take action? • Parents and carers of children and young people under the age of 16. • Local authorities (providers of cultural and leisure services). • Schools (governors and teachers). • Catering staff. • Nursery nurses and workers in pre-school day care settings such as nurseries. • Managers of children's centres. What action should they take? • Help children and young people to have a healthy diet
