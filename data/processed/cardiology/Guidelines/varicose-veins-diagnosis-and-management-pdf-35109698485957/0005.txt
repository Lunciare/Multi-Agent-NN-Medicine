Making decisions using NICE guidelines explains how we use words to show the strength (or certainty) of our recommendations, and has information about prescribing medicines (including off-label use), professional guidelines, standards and laws (including on consent and mental capacity), and safeguarding. 1.1 Information for people with varicose veins 1.1.1 Give people who present with varicose veins information that includes: • An explanation of what varicose veins are. • Possible causes of varicose veins. • The likelihood of progression and possible complications, including deep vein thrombosis, skin changes, leg ulcers, bleeding and thrombophlebitis.
