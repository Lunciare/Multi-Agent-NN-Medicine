A vascular service is a team of healthcare professionals who have the skills to undertake a full clinical and duplex ultrasound assessment and provide a full range of treatment. 1.2 Referral to a vascular service 1.2.1 Refer people with bleeding varicose veins to a vascular service immediately. 1.2.2 Refer people to a vascular service if they have any of the following. • Symptomatic primary or symptomatic recurrent varicose veins. Symptomatic veins are veins found in association with troublesome lower limb symptoms (typically pain, aching, discomfort, swelling, heaviness and itching). • Lower-limb skin changes, such as pigmentation or eczema, thought to be caused by chronic venous insufficiency. •
