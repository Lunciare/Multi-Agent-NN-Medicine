The manufacturer performed a literature search to assess the relationship between utility values in the PLATO study and in the literature. The lower values from the literature were used in sensitivity analyses. The utility scores from both the PLATO-HECON substudy and the literature were adjusted downwards by 0.0328 to better reflect the patient population that would be treated in UK clinical practice.
