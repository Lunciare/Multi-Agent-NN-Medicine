The primary care trust expert noted that this would invariably lead to reduced spending elsewhere for health, which would include cardiology services. The Committee noted further comments received from consultees that affordability was an issue that NHS commissioners needed to consider 'very seriously'. Although the Committee agreed that that budget impact would be substantial, it was possible that any services displaced might be less cost effective than ticagrelor relative to clopidogrel.
