Other relevant literature is in the appendix of the overview. 3.2 The professional experts and the committee considered the key efficacy outcomes to be: clot removal, recovery of cardiac function, 30-day mortality or survival, symptomatic relief, long-term patient-reported outcome measures, and quality of life. 3.3 The professional experts and the committee considered the key safety outcomes to be: pain, bleeding, damage to vessels and the heart, and worsening cardiac function. 3.4 One commentary from a person who had this procedure was received as part of consultation comments and was discussed by the committee.
