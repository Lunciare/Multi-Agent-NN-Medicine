Glossary Hypertension: Hypertension is a condition where the blood pressure in the arteries is persistently high. Normal blood pressure is below 120/70 mmHg. Hypertension is diagnosed when readings are consistently 140/90 mmHg or higher. Systolic pressure: This is the top number in a blood pressure reading. It measures the pressure in your arteries when your heart contracts. Diastolic pressure: This is the bottom number in a blood pressure reading. It measures the pressure in your arteries when your heart relaxes between beats. ACE
