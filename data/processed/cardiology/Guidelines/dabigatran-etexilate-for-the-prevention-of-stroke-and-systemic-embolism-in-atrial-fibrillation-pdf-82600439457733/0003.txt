Concomitant treatment with systemic ketoconazole, cyclosporine, itraconazole or tacrolimus is also contraindicated. The most common adverse events in people receiving dabigatran are anaemia, abdominal pain, diarrhoea, dyspepsia, gastrointestinal haemorrhage, genitourinary haemorrhage (patients may notice blood in their urine), nausea and nose bleeds. For full details of adverse reactions and contraindications, see the summary of product characteristics. 2.4 Dabigatran is available as 110 mg and 150 mg capsules and comes in packs of 60 capsules.
