The device is also wire free and is water resistant, meaning that it can be worn in the shower and during exercise. However, users are advised to avoid showering or strenuous exercise for at least 24 hours after applying the patch. They also recommend avoiding environments or activities that lead to excessive sweating and advise against any activity which involves fully submerging the device in water, such as swimming or submerging the device whilst bathing. Current care pathway The CAM is intended for use in those who need extended duration cardiac monitoring for people with suspected cardiac arrhythmias. NICE's guideline on atrial fibrillation states that standard care for people with suspected arrhythmias is assessment using manual pulse palpation followed by an ECG. In people with suspected paroxysmal atrial fibrillation undetected by standard ECG recording, use of a 24-hour ambulatory ECG monitor is recommended in people with suspected episodes less than 24 hours apart.
