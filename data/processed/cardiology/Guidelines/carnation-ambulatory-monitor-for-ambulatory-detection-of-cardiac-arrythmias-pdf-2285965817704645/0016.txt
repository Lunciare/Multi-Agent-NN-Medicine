Expert comments Comments on this technology were invited from clinical experts working in the field and relevant patient organisations. The comments received are individual opinions and do not represent NICE's view. All 3 of the experts were familiar with or had used this technology before. Level of innovation All experts acknowledged that this technology was not as innovative as other ambulatory cardiac monitors available.
