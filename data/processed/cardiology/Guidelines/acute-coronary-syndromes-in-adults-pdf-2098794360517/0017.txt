Structure Evidence of local arrangements to ensure that adults who are unconscious after cardiac arrest caused by suspected acute STEMI are not excluded from having coronary angiography (with follow-on primary PCI if indicated) because they are unconscious. Data source: Local data collection. Process Proportion of adults who were unconscious after cardiac arrest caused by suspected acute STEMI who receive coronary angiography (with follow-on primary PCI if indicated). Numerator – the number in the denominator receiving coronary angiography (with follow- on primary PCI if indicated). Denominator – the number of adults who were unconscious after cardiac arrest caused by suspected acute STEMI. Data source: Local data collection.
