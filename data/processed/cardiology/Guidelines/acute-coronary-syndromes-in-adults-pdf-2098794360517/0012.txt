[NICE's guideline on acute coronary syndromes, recommendation 1.2.13] Quality statement 4: Coronary angiography and PCI for adults with NSTEMI or unstable angina who are clinically unstable Quality statement Adults with non-ST-segment-elevation myocardial infarction (NSTEMI) or unstable angina who are clinically unstable have coronary angiography (with follow-on percutaneous coronary intervention [PCI] if indicated) as soon as possible, but within 24 hours of becoming clinically unstable.
