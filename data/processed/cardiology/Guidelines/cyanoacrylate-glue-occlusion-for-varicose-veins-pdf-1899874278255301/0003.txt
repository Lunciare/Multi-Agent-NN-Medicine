Other relevant literature is in the appendix of the overview. 3.2 The specialist advisers and the committee considered the key efficacy outcomes to be: saphenous vein occlusion rate, recanalisation, symptom relief and quality of life. 3.3 The specialist advisers and the committee considered the key safety outcomes to be: hypersensitivity, granuloma formation, thromboembolism, and nerve injury or paraesthesia. 3.4 Three commentaries from patients who have had this procedure were discussed by the committee.
