The Committee considered that total cross- clamp time and long-term graft performance are important outcome measures. 3 Further information 3.1 NICE has issued interventional procedures guidance on stent-graft placement in abdominal aortic aneurysm and endovascular stent-graft placement in thoracic aortic aneurysms and dissections.
