Recommendations People have the right to be involved in discussions and make informed decisions about their care, as described in NICE's information on making decisions about your care. Making decisions using NICE guidelines explains how we use words to show the strength (or certainty) of our recommendations, and has information about prescribing medicines (including off-label use), professional guidelines, standards and laws (including on consent and mental capacity), and safeguarding. This guideline constitutes NICE's formal guidance on what works in finding and supporting those most at risk of early death and improving their access to services. The evidence statements that underpin the recommendations are listed in appendix C.
