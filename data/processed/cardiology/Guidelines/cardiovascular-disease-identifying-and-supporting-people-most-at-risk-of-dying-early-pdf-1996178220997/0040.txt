Summarising the evidence and making evidence statements The review data was summarised in evidence tables (see the full evidence reviews). The findings from the reviews were synthesised and used as the basis for a number of evidence statements relating to each key question. The evidence statements reflect the strength (quantity, type and quality) of evidence and its applicability to the populations and settings in the scope.
