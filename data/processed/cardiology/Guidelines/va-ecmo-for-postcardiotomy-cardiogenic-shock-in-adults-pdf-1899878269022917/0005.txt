The evidence 3.4 NICE did a rapid review of the published literature on the efficacy and safety of this procedure. This comprised a comprehensive literature search and detailed review of the evidence from 12 sources (13 publications), which was discussed by the committee. The evidence included 4 systematic reviews, 3 retrospective registry studies, 1 multicentre retrospective study and 4 single centre retrospective studies. It is presented in the summary of key evidence section in the interventional procedures overview.
