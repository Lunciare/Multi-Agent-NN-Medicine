Equality considerations 3.16 Not all cardiac surgery centres specialise in using VA ECMO in the UK. So, people in some areas may not have access to this procedure. 3.17 Pregnancy and maternity are protected characteristics under the Equality Act (2010). Women, trans men and non-binary people with cardiogenic shock who are pregnant, or who have recently been pregnant, may need to access this intervention. 4 Committee members and NICE project team This topic was considered by NICE's interventional procedures advisory committee, which is a standing advisory committee of NICE.
