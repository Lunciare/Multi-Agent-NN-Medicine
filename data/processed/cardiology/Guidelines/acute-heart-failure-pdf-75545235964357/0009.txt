NICE guideline CG187, recommendations 1.2.3 (key priority for implementation) and 1.2.4 (key priority for implementation) Definition of terms used in this quality statement Transthoracic doppler 2D echocardiogram An echocardiogram is a test that uses ultrasound waves to measure the pumping action and structure of the heart, including the heart valves. A probe is moved over the surface of the chest and picks up echoes of sound (similar to an ultrasound scan used in pregnancy), which are shown as a picture on a screen.
