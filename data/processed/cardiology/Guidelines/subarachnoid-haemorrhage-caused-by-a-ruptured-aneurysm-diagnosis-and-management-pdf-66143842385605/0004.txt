Pain relief and neurological assessment 1.1.8 Ensure that people with a suspected or confirmed subarachnoid haemorrhage are given effective pain relief, including opioid analgesia if needed. Document administration of opioid analgesia in the person's healthcare record. 1.1.9 When conducting a neurological assessment, check the person's care record and if opioid analgesia has been given, take into account its sedating and pupillary effects.
