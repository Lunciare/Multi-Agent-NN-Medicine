A Acknowledge: you and your healthcare provider should acknowledge (recognise or admit) that there are strong links between CVD and mental health C Check: your healthcare team (e.g. doctor, nurse, psychologist) should check regularly how you are feeling during your visits for cardiovascular problems Tools: it is important that the right tools (checklists, questionnaires, etc.) are used T to check your mental well-being. If you think that no tool has been used by your healthcare provider to assess your psychological status, then ask them I Implement: let us work together to make this happen. Sharing decisions and the stepped care approach are needed to put changes in place V Venture: joint mental and cardiovascular care is often not received. It is key that patients and professionals dare to change the system to make this happen E Evaluate: things can change, but changes need to be evaluated by you and your healthcare team to be sure that mental health care is changing for the better How to work with your Psycho-Cardio team: • Be open about your symptoms and feelings – voice your concerns • Work with the team and inform them about what you need and like –
