If you have CVD, you may be at greater risk of developing a mental health condition, such as anxiety or depression. Screening for mental health conditions is possible and important Some mental health conditions may be missed when patients with CVD visit the doctor or hospital. The specialist may think that feelings like worry or fear are just a normal reaction to the CVD. These feelings might be normal at first, but if they last for a long time or get stronger, they can cause problems.
