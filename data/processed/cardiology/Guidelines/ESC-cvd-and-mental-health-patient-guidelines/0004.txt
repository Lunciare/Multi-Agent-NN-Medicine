The Psycho-Cardio team If you have a CVD and/or mental health condition, you can benefit from integrated, person-centred care. The ESC has recommended the creation of Psycho-Cardio teams to improve the care of mental health conditions among people with CVD. Psycho-Cardio teams are composed of multiple specialists (including nurses, doctors, psychologists, psychiatrists) who work together for the best cardiovascular and mental health care for patients.
