LDL cholesterol results may not be reported in participants with triglyceride levels more than 4.5 mmol per litre or 9 mmol per litre depending on the formula used by local laboratories. High-intensity statin The following doses for statins are high intensity, based on the percentage reduction in LDL cholesterol they can produce: • atorvastatin: 20 mg to 80 mg • rosuvastatin: 10 mg to 40 mg. Medium-intensity statin The following doses for statins are medium intensity, based on the percentage reduction in LDL cholesterol they can produce: • atorvastatin: 10 mg • fluvastatin: 80 mg • rosuvastatin: 5 mg • simvastatin: 20 mg to 40 mg. Low-intensity statin The following doses for statins are low intensity, based on the percentage reduction in LDL cholesterol they can produce: • fluvastatin: 20 mg to 40 mg • pravastatin: 5 mg to 40 mg • simvastatin: 10 mg.
