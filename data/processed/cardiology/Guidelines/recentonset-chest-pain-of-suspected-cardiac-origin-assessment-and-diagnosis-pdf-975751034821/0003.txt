[2010] 1.2 People presenting with acute chest pain This section of the guideline covers the assessment and diagnosis of people with recent acute chest pain or discomfort, suspected to be caused by an acute coronary syndrome (ACS). The term ACS covers a range of conditions including unstable angina, ST-segment- elevation myocardial infarction (STEMI) and non-ST-segment-elevation myocardial infarction (NSTEMI). The guideline addresses assessment and diagnosis irrespective of setting, because people present in different ways. For early management of these conditions, follow the NICE guideline on acute coronary syndromes. 1.2.1 Initial assessment and referral to hospital 1.2.1.1 Check immediately whether people currently have chest pain.
