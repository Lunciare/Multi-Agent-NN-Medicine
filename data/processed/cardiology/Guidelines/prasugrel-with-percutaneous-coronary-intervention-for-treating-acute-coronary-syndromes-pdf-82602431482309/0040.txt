The manufacturer of clopidogrel carried out extensive re-analyses of the CAPRIE trial data as requested by the Assessment Group in order to estimate independent event hazards adjusted to age, sex and event history. 4.2.16 The Assessment Group's economic model included costs calculated from the pack price for 28 tablets of clopidogrel, prasugrel and low-dose aspirin as stated in the NHS Drug Tariff (November 2013; clopidogrel and low-dose aspirin) and the BNF (October 2013, edition 66; prasugrel). Clopidogrel has a pack price of £1.71, giving a cost of £0.24 per loading dose, £18.43 for a 12-month supply (adjusted for treatment duration), and £29.37 for the total dual antiplatelet therapy cost in year 1. Prasugrel has a pack price of £47.56, giving a cost of £10.19 per loading dose, £511.67 for a 12-month supply (adjusted for treatment duration) and £532.56 for the total dual antiplatelet therapy cost in year 1.
