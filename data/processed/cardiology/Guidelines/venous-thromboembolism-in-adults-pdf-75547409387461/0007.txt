[NICE's guideline on venous thromboembolism in over 16s, terms used in this guideline] Equality and diversity considerations The supporting information for this statement highlights that people should be given verbal and written information on using VTE prophylaxis correctly and the possible side effects. Information should be in a format that suits their needs and preferences. It should be accessible to people who do not speak or read English, and it should be culturally appropriate and age appropriate. For people with additional needs related to a disability, impairment or sensory loss, information should also be provided as set out in NHS England's Accessible Information Standard or the equivalent standards for the devolved nations.
