Peopleaged 16 and over who are in hospital and who need medicine to prevent blood clots start taking the medicine within 14 hours of being admitted to hospital. People who have a very low or high body weight or whose kidney function is impaired have the dose of medicine they are given adjusted. They discuss the medicine with a healthcare professional and make decisions about taking it. Their healthcare professional explains and gives them written information about how to use the medicine and any possible side effects.
