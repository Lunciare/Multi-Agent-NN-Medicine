It also took into account the effective use of NHS resources. Clinical effectiveness 4.2 The committee considered the clinical need for treatment in people with heart failure who are covered by the marketing authorisation of ivabradine. The committee noted that the clinical specialists indicated that ivabradine is primarily a heart-rate-lowering drug for people with left ventricular systolic dysfunction who are in sinus rhythm and for whom beta-blockers are not suitable. The committee heard from the clinical specialists that people with chronic heart failure have a poor quality of life.
