Catheter-based interventions include balloon angioplasty with or without stenting, atherectomy, intravascular lithotripsy, thrombectomy, and brachytherapy to mechanically manage and stabilize plaques or remove thrombi. Surgical interventions aim to bypass (bypass grafting) or remove (endarterectomy) severe atherosclerotic lesions, while aneurysm repair with synthetic grafts is performed to prevent rupture.
