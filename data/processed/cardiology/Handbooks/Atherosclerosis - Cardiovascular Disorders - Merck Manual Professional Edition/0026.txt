Coronary flow reserve (CFR):-This technique uses a Doppler or a pressure-temperature sensor-tipped guidewire to assess the functional significance of coronary artery disease and microvascular disease by measuring the ratio of maximal flow in a coronary artery to the flow under resting conditions. Endothelial function testing: A vasoactive agent (eg, acetylcholine) is injected into the coronary arteries to provoke vasospasm and assess the endothelial response.
