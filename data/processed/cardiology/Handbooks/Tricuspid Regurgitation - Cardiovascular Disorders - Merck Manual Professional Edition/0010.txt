Tricuspid Regurgitation
KEYWORDS: tricuspid, valve, regurgitation, tricuspid regurgitation, severe, heart, right, atrial, repair, murmur, replacement, normal, secondary, patients, usually

include a holosystolic murmur heard best at the left middle or lower sternal border or at the epigastrium when the patient is sitting upright or standing; the murmur becomes louder with inspiration. TR is usually well tolerated, but severe cases may require annuloplasty, valve repair, or valve replacement.
