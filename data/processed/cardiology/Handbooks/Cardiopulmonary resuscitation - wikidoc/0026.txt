As well as causing significant local trauma, in theory performing CPR on healthy persons may disrupt heart rhythms. Application on animals It is entirely feasible to perform CPR on animals like cats and dogs. The principles and practices are virtually identical to CPR for humans. One is cautioned to only perform CPR on unconscious animals to avoid the risk of being bitten.
