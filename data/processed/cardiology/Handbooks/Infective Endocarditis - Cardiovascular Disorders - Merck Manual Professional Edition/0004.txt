The disease develops in 3 stages: Bacteremia: Microorganisms are present in the blood Adhesion: The microorganism adheres to abnormal or damaged endothelium via surface adhesions Colonization: Proliferation of the organism together with inflammation, leading to a mature vegetation Many of the causative microorganisms produce polysaccharide biofilms that shield them from host immune defences and impede antibiotic penetration.
