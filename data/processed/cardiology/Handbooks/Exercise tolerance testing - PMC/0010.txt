Prognostic testing Exercise testing in patients who have just had a myocardial infarction is indicated only in those in whom a revascularisation procedure is contemplated; a less strenuous protocol is used. Testing provides prognostic information. Patients with low exercise capacity and hypotension induced by exercise have a poor prognosis.
