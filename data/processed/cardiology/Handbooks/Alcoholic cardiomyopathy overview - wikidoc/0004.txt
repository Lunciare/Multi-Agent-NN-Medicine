Treatment Medical Therapy 2/3 Treatment for alcoholic cardiomyopathy involves lifestyle changes, including complete abstinence from alcohol use [5][6][7][8], a low sodium diet, and fluid restriction, as well as medications. If the heart failure is severe, the effectiveness of treatment will be limited. Surgery In severe or unresponsive patients, cardiac transplantation can be an option.
