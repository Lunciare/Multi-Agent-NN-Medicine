Medication - diuretics, ACE inhibitors, angiotensin-II receptor blockers, beta- blockers, mineralocorticoid receptor antagonists. ECG Arrange an ECG in all patients with suspected heart failure. This not only identifies potential aetiological factors (for example, myocardial infarction or arrhythmias) but is also necessary for treatment decisions - eg, rate control and anticoagulation for AF or pacing for bradycardia.
