White arrows indicate normal blood flow. (Tricuspid valve labeled at bottom left.) Pathology A small amount of leakage or regurgitation is not uncommon in the tricuspid valve. It is a common valve to be infected (endocarditis) in IV drug users.[1][2] Although it is not a common site of endocarditis, patients with a small VSD usually develop endocarditis of the tricuspid valve.
