Nonatheromatous Arteriosclerosis
KEYWORDS: arteriosclerosis, arteriolosclerosis, nonatheromatous, medial, hypertension, nonatheromatous arteriosclerosis, arterial, calcific, diabetes, medial calcific, thickening, calcification, sclerosis, calcific sclerosis, wall

Nonatheromatous arteriosclerosis is age-related loss of elasticity due to fibrosis or calcification in the aorta and its major branches. No specific treatment is available. Arteriosclerosis is a general term for several disorders that cause thickening and loss of elasticity in the arterial wall. Atherosclerosis is discussed separately.
