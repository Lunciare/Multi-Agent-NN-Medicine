When added to ACE inhibitor or ARB therapy, hydralazine plus nitrate therapy is started at 37.5 mg and isosorbide dinitrate at 20 mg orally 3 times a day, with the maximum dose 75 mg and 40 mg 3 times a day. These doses are also available as a fixed-dose combination. Nitrates and phosphodiesterase-5 inhibitors have been used to relieve symptoms in patients with HFpEF, but neither is recommended for routine use for these patients (2).
