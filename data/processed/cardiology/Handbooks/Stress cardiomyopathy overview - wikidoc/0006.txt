Stress cardiomyopathy overview
KEYWORDS: cardiomyopathy, stress, stress cardiomyopathy, findings, patients, disease, patients stress, coronary, medical, include, physical, complications, diagnosis, echocardiography, conditions

Prevention There are no means of prevention of stress cardiomyopathy. Secondary Prevention Stress cardiomyopathy is a self-limiting disease, which usually resolves within a few weeks. However, medical therapy may be necessary during the acute phase of the illness or when complications arise.
