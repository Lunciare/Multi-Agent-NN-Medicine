Treatment of Rheumatic Fever Antibiotics Aspirin Sometimes corticosteroids The primary goals of rheumatic fever treatment are eradication of group A streptococcal infection, relief of acute symptoms, suppression of inflammation, and prophylaxis against future infection to prevent recurrent heart disease.
