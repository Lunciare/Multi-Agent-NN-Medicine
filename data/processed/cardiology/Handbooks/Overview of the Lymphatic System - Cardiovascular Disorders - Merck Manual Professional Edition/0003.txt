Overview of the Lymphatic System
KEYWORDS: lymphatic, lymph, nodes, lymph nodes, cancer, cells, primary, infection, fluid, venous, inflammation, immune, rarely, cellular, local

organisms spread through the lymphatic system from the primary site of infection. Various cancers may metastasize to local or regional lymph nodes. Rarely, a primary cancer (eg, lymphangiosarcoma) develops in the lymphatic system.
