Chapter 12 Aneurysm and dissection of the aorta Aortic aneurysm Aneurysms of the thoracic aorta (see Figure 1) are not as common as those affecting the abdominal portion, but carry a higher risk of rupture. Both share the same primary cause: atherosclerosis.
