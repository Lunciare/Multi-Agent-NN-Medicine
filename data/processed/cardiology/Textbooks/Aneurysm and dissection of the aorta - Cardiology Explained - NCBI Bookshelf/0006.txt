If Î²-blockers are contraindicated then rate-limiting calcium-channel blockers, such as diltiazem or verapamil, can be used. Dissection typically presents with a severe tearing central chest pain that radiates to the back. A patient with such a history should be referred urgently to a specialist center since the ascending aorta might be involved.
