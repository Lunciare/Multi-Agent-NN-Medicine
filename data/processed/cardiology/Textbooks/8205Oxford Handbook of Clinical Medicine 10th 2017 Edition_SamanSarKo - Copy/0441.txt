Legionella pneumophila Colonizes water tanks kept at <60°C (eg hotel air-con- ditioning and hot water systems) causing outbreaks. Flu-like symptoms (fever, malaise, myalgia) precede a dry cough and dyspnoea. Extra-pulmonary features in- clude anorexia, D&V, hepatitis, renal failure, confusion, and coma. CXR shows bi-basal consolidation. Blood tests may show lymphopenia, hyponatraemia, and deranged LFTS. Urinalysis may show haematuria. Diagnosis: Urine antigen/culture.
