Treatment10 depends upon whether the disease is uncomplicated, or severe. Features of severe disease are: • Impaired consciousness/seizures (consider LP) • Hb <80g/L • AKI (oliguria <0.4mL/kg/h, creatinine>265μmol/L) • Spontaneous bleeding/DIC • Shock (BP <90/60) = ‘Algid malaria’ • Acidosis (pH <7.3) • Hypoglycaemia (<2.2mmol/L) • Haemoglobinuria • Pulmonary oedema/ARDS • Parasitaemia >10%.
