Insertion care bundle 1 Aseptic technique. 2 Hand hygiene. 3 Apron + non-sterile gloves. 4 Skin prepara- tion—2% chorhexidine in 70% isopropyl alcohol (allow to dry for 30 seconds). Do not repalpate vein after cleaning unless wearing sterile gloves. 5 Dressing—sterile and transparent so that insertion site can be observed. After insertion 1 Take blood with syringe or adaptor. 2 Remove tourniquet. 3 Attach needle-free de- vice (if appropriate) and fl ush with 10mL 0.9% saline. 4 Apply dressing. 5 Let nursing staff know that cannula is in place and ready for use. 6 Document insertion according to local policy. 7 Write up appropriate fl uids or parenteral medication.
