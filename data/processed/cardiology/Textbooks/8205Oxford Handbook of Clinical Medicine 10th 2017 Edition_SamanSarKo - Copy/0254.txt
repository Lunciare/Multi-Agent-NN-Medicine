Ask specifi cally about suicidal thoughts and plans: ‘Have you ever been so low that you thought of harming yourself?’, ‘What thoughts have you had?’ Check for hypomanic and manic features which can be missed in a patient presenting as depressed. Hallucinations (‘Have you ever heard voices or seen things when there hasn’t been anyone or anything there?’) and delusions (‘Have you ever had any thoughts or beliefs that have struck you afterwards as bizarre?’); anxiety and avoidance behaviour (eg avoiding shopping because of anxiety or phobias); ob- sessional thoughts and compulsive behaviour, eating disorders, alcohol (see p281 for alcohol screening tests) and other drugs.
