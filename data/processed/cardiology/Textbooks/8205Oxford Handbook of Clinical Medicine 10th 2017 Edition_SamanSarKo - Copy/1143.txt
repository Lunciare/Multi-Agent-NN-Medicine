Trachoma Leading infectious cause of blindness worldwide: visual impairment/blindness in 1.9 million, 200 million at risk. Prevalence in endemic areas 60- 90% (Africa, Central and South America, Asia, Middle East). Caused by Chlamydia trachomatis. Human-to- human transmission with contact, or via fl ies which land on noses/eyes.
