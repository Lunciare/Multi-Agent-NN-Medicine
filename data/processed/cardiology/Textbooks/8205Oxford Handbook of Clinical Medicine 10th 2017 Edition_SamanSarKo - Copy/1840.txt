Alexander Tietze, 1864–1927 (German surgeon) Todd’s palsy Transient neurological defi cit (paresis) after a seizure. There may be face, arm, or leg weakness, aphasia, or gaze palsy, lasting from ~30min–36h. The aetiology is unclear. Jean Hyacinthe Vincent, 1862–1950 (French physician) Von Hippel–Lindau syndrome A dominant germline mutation of a tumour sup- pressor gene.
