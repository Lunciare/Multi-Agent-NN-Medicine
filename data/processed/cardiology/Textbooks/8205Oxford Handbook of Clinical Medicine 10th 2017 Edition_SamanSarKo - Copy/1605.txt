Polyps, the challenges of screening, and the NHS Polyps are growths that appear above the mucosa and can be infl ammatory, hamartomatous, or neoplastic. Left in situ, polyps carry a risk of malignant trans- formation that will relate to size and histology (tubular or villous adenomas, esp. if >2cm).
