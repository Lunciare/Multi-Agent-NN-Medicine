Suggest: 1 Graceful ways of declining a drink, eg ‘I’m seeing what it’s like to go without for a bit’. 2 Not buying him- or herself a drink when it is his/her turn. 3 ‘Don’t lift your glass to your lips until after the slowest drinker in your group takes a drink.’ 4 ‘Sip, don’t gulp.’ Give follow-up and encouragement.
