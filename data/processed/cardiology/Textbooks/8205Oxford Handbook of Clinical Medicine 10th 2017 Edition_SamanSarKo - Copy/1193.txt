Drug history Exclude medication overuse (analgesic rebound) headache: Culprits are mixed analgesics (paracetamol + codeine/opiates), ergotamine, and triptans. This is a common reason for episodic headache becoming chronic daily headache. Anal- gesia must be withdrawn—aspirin or naproxen may mollify the rebound headache. A preventive may help once off other drugs (eg tricyclics, valproate, g abapentin; p504). Limit use of over-the-counter analgesia (no more than 6d per month).
