Needle-stick injury Risk of HIV transmission from a single needle-stick exposure from a person with HIV not on ART is ~1 in 300 (lower than risks of hepatitis B and C transmission). Prevent: • Use ‘safer sharps’ (incorporates a mechanism to minimize accidental injury). • Do not recap unprotected medical sharps. • When using sharps, ensure there is a disposal container nearby.
