Ageing is an inevitable and irreversible decline in organ function that occurs with time, in the absence of injury or illness, and despite the existence of complex path- ways of maintenance and repair. Healthy ageing is the maintenance of physical and mental abilities that enable well- being and independence in older age. Do not presume ageing. Look for preventable and reversible pathology. Old age does not cause disease (although it can increase vulnerability and recovery time). Look for ways to reduce disability and support older people in their own homes.
