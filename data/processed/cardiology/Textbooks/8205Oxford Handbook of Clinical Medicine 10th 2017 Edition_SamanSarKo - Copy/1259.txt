In distinguishing the two conditions (not always an easy task) the presence of inattention, distractibility, and disorganized thinking will all point you towards delirium. But the fundamental question is ‘Has there been an acute change from the patient’s cognitive baseline?’ Family or carer collateral reports are invalu- able, but may not always be available. Document cognition in all patients >65y admitted to hospital (eg AMTs, p64, many admission proformas allow for this).
