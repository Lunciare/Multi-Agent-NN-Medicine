Many of the diseases in this chapter cause multisystem pathology. For these infections, it may be helpful to classify by pathogen (table 9.1). However, infectious agents do not walk in the door and introduce themselves. Detective work may be necessary based on geography; or exposure: to vectors, animals, and con- taminated water/food. And so other pages in this chapter have that as their (helpful) premise. When infection is organ specifi c, you may need to look elsewhere (table 9.2).
