It was recorded from Thomas Goswell, a technician in the laboratory, using a capillary electrometer. Depolarization of the heart The route that the depolarization wave takes across the heart is outlined in Figure 3. The sinoatrial node (SAN) is the heart's pacemaker. From the SAN, the wave of depolarization 2/9 spreads across the atria to the atrioventricular node (AVN). The impulse is delayed briefly at the AVN and atrial contraction is completed.
