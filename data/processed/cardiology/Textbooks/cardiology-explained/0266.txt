Overall death rates are 20% for native valve endocarditis, 30% for staphylococcal infections, and 20%–30% for late prosthetic valve infection, despite full medical and surgical treatment. The most common cause of death is intractable heart failure. Prophylaxis All patients at risk for IE should receive antibiotic cover for invasive procedures (see Tables 2 and 3, overleaf).
