Between the two posterior horns of gray matter are the posterior columns. Between the two anterior horns, and bounded by the axons of motor neurons emerging from that gray matter area, are theanterior columns. The white matter on either side of the spinal cord, between the posterior horn and the axons of the anterior horn neurons, are thelateral columns. The posterior columns are composed of axons of ascending tracts.
