Recent studies are also finding a link between insufficient vitamin D and cancer. 5.4 Diseases, Disorders, and Injuries of the Integumentary System LEARNING OBJECTIVES By the end of this section, you will be able to: • Describe several different diseases and disorders of the skin • Describe the effect of injury to the skin and the process of healing The integumentary system is susceptible to a variety of diseases, disorders, and injuries. These range from annoying but relatively benign bacterial or fungal infections that are categorized as disorders, to skin cancer and severe burns, which can be fatal. In this section, you will learn several of the most common skin conditions. Diseases One of the most talked about diseases is skin cancer.
