technical positions: • Medical technologists (MT), also known as clinical laboratory technologists (CLT), typically hold a bachelor’s degree and certification from an accredited training program. They perform a wide variety of tests on various body fluids, including blood. The information they provide is essential to the primary care providers in determining a diagnosis and in monitoring the course of a disease and response to treatment. • Medical laboratory technicians (MLT) typically have an associate’s degree but may perform duties similar to those of an MT. • Medical laboratory assistants (MLA) spend the majority of their time processing samples and carrying out routine assignments within the lab.
