The Frequency of Motor Neuron Stimulation A single action potential from a motor neuron will produce a single contraction in the muscle fibers of its motor unit. This isolated contraction is called atwitch. A twitch can last for a few milliseconds or 100 milliseconds, depending on the muscle type.
